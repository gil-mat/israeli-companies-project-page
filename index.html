01 Vision

The Future Of Business Discovery

"Websites were built for humans.
The Companies Project is built for AI."

Business information is fragmented across websites, PDFs, CRMs, marketplaces, and databases. The signal that defines a company is scattered, unstructured, and impossible to trust at scale.

AI systems struggle to understand who companies are, what they offer, and how they should connect — because the world's business data was never designed to be read by machines.

The Companies Project aims to solve this by creating a structured, verified, AI-readable network of companies — a single source of truth where intelligent systems can reason about the global economy.

02 Platform

How The Future Network Works

Four layers turn fragmented business data into a living, verifiable network that humans and AI agents can both navigate.

01 / PUBLISH

Publish

Companies create rich, structured profiles designed for both humans and AI systems to read, parse, and reason about.

02 / VERIFY

Verify

Identity, ownership, and trust layers improve business reliability and make counterparties safe to transact with.

03 / DISCOVER

Discover

AI agents identify opportunities, suppliers, customers, and partners automatically — across borders, in real time.

04 / CONNECT

Connect

Businesses engage through AI-assisted workflows that reduce friction, compress timelines, and increase speed.
